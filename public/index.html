FAQ

Questions parents ask

What ages is this for? ▼
Kindergarten through 8th grade. Elementary and Middle School groups run separately. A dedicated Kindergarten–1st grade group will be added depending on sign-up volume.
Are sessions split by age? ▼
Yes. Elementary and Middle School groups run separately. A dedicated Kindergarten–1st grade group will be added depending on sign-up volume. Dedicated coaches for each group starting next season.
Does my child need prior experience? ▼
No. Beginners are absolutely welcome. Qumtak is designed to build fundamentals from the ground up.
How do I know what's happening each week? ▼
Check this site — we post the full weekly session plan every week so you know exactly what's happening.
Are there tryouts? ▼
No tryouts. Ever. Qumtak is open enrollment — any child in the age range can sign up. We believe elite coaching should be accessible to every kid.
How do I register? ▼
Group classes in Andover are registered through the Andover Recreation Department on MyRec. Click Enroll on this page. For private training, book through the link above or email noah@qmtk.org.
What should my child bring? ▼
Soccer cleats or athletic shoes, shin guards, a ball (extras available), and water. Dress for the weather — sessions run rain or shine unless fields are unsafe.
Do sessions include scrimmages? ▼
Yes. Every session ends with competitive scrimmage time — real game situations with goalies and proper goals. The best way to develop is to play.
Is Qumtak coming to Reading? ▼
Yes. Qumtak is actively working to launch sessions in Reading. Interested Reading parents or rec officials should reach out to noah@qmtk.org.
Where are classes held? ▼
Spring 2026 indoor classes (final session Sat Apr 19) are at Old Shawsheen School Gymnasium in Andover. Outdoor classes (Apr 27–Jun 12, Mon & Fri) are at Sanborn Elementary School — Lower Field, Andover. Private training locations are flexible. Reading-area sessions are coming soon.
How much does it cost? ▼
Outdoor spring 2026 group classes are $260 for Andover residents and $275 for non-residents — covering the full outdoor session (Apr 27–Jun 12, Mon & Fri 5:50–6:50 PM at Sanborn Elementary). Private training rates available upon inquiry.
